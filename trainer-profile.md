# **Trainer Profile — Vivek Anandaraman**

**Vivek Anandaraman**
*SRE & FinOps Consultant | Founder, CloudElu Labs | Organizer, From-Dev-to-Ops Community*
Chennai, India

Vivek is a practitioner-first SRE/FinOps consultant helping engineering and product teams embed reliability, observability, and cloud cost awareness directly into their DevOps workflows. He specializes in enabling modern engineering teams to make data-driven decisions on cloud reliability, spend, and performance—without slowing down developers.

With 20+ years of experience across SRE, cloud infrastructure, performance engineering, and analytics, Vivek brings a unique blend of business thinking and technical execution. He has led large-scale SRE transformations, founded community initiatives, mentored DevOps talent, and worked directly with CTOs, architects, and engineering leads to operationalize modern cloud practices across AWS, Azure, and GCP.

In 2024, Vivek founded **CloudElu Labs**, a consulting and enablement initiative focused on FinOps and SRE as a Service. He also organizes the **From-Dev-to-Ops** community in Chennai, connecting learners with industry practitioners to accelerate knowledge sharing in DevOps and platform engineering.

Previously, Vivek spent 13+ years at Cognizant as Principal Architect leading strategic programs in observability, cloud cost optimization, capacity planning, and SRE. He helped enterprise customers modernize reliability engineering practices, implement OpenTelemetry-based observability architectures, automate workflows, and manage SRE teams responsible for uptime and performance of mission-critical systems.
